Version 2.3.1 has been published to Stable tModLoader v2025.2

修复了在高频点击鼠标时攻击速度异常加快的问题。
该问题是由于攻击动画尚未结束时模组切回初始武器，导致玩家可以绕过攻击冷却。现在动画未结束时不会立即切回主武器，动画结束后才会自动还原。

-------------------------------------------------

Fixed an issue where rapidly clicking the mouse caused whip attacks to become abnormally fast.
This was caused by switching back to the initial weapon before the attack animation finished, allowing players to bypass cooldown. Now the weapon only switches back after the animation ends.
